Welcome to Paducah Coffee Company.

We are western Kentucky's premier specialty coffee roasters. Since 2015, we have provided our clients with the finest coffees; imported from all over the world, and roasted right here in Paducah. By roasting only to order, and carefully selecting our green coffees for environmental impact and fair trade practices, we are doing our part to make both Paducah and the world a better place each day. Start your morning with a cup (or a whole pot) of caffeinated excellence from your friendly neighborhood coffee roasters.
